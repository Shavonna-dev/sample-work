About the Neverending Mall





The Neverending Mall was founded in 1970 by J.P. Neverending, a visonary who saw that one day, people would flock to an area that contained all the stores they wanted to go to all in one place, a one stop shopping mall. Inspired by an idea given to him by his young imaginative grandson, he set out to create one of the biggest shopping malls ever, one that almost never ends. Located near Chicago's Downtown Shopping District, just a half mile down from the Magnificent Mile, the Neverending Mall offers everything you need for your holiday season, and for everyday life stuff as well.
