Welcome to Claude Monet Website!

Monet was born Nov. 14, 1840 Paris France Dec. 5, 1926 French painter, initiator, leader, and unswerving advocate of the Impressionist style. You are about to embark his trail of his precious art. Enjoy :)

 

First Impressionist

In 1871 and 1872 he painted canals, boats, and windmills in Holland and worked again at Le Havre.

Last Impressionist

In 1875 and 1876 he painted oil canvasses on women in The Seine at the Argenteuil.
